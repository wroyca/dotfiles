{MiniFilesGitignoreConfig}

{prefetch_depth} `(number)` Depth for prefetching subdirectories (default: 2)
{enable_metrics} `(boolean)` Enable performance metrics (default: false)
{log_level} `(number)` Logging level (default: vim.log.levels.WARN)
